Claudio Javier de la Cruz

Claudio Javier de la Cruz traveled solo for several months in 2013 through much of Southeast Asia. He lived in Malapascua for two weeks, and the photos you see below are from his stay there.

Since 2008, diving has been one of his passions. With nearly 150 dives, he has dived in the Mediterranean, the Canary Islands, Mexico, Cuba, Thailand, the Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia, the Maldives, and the Red Sea.

His stay on the island of Malapascua not only allowed him to explore its sea beds but also served as inspiration for his work Malapascua, Isla Maldita.
